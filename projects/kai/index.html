Kai Smokehouse

Wix Website Builder

The timeframe for this project was short, so I deemed it best to use the service Wix, an online website builder. This decision gave me a chance to flaunt my design and creative skills. Using their system I was easily able to create a gorgeous looking site including an online booking system paired with Table Agent.

As always I strived to keep the theme of the site true to the experience of dining in Kai, even down to the colour scheme, which I feel I achieved. Though the restaurant were extremely pleased, they have since gone out of business and taken down the site.

My work on the site can be found - https://kaismokehouse.wixsite.com/kaismokehouse
