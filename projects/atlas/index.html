Atlas

Laravel & JQuery

The core group of Atlas is a group I have worked with closely during my time as marketing director for REACH for Autism. This group aims to spread the word of autism and provides training for businesses and organisations reaching from police scotland to the first minister of Scotland, Nicola Sturgeon.

Features inlude:

  • Dynamically served 'Services' content
  • Laravel Mailer for contact form
  • Text resizer for accessability
  • High contrast site for autistic users with site problems
